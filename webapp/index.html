TriCoach AI

Personal AI coach for triathletes. Analyzes HRV, training load and recovery — delivers actionable recommendations every morning.

💬 Open in Telegram

Features

🟢

Morning Report

Recovery score, dual-algorithm HRV analysis (Flatt & Esco + AIEndurance), sleep quality and RHR. AI-powered workout recommendation.

📈

Training Load

CTL / ATL / TSB from Intervals.icu. Per-sport CTL for swim, bike and run. Ramp rate monitoring.

❤️‍🔥

DFA Alpha 1

Post-activity HRV analysis: HRVT1/HRVT2 thresholds, Readiness (Ra), Durability (Da). Automatically processed from FIT files.

🏁

Race Progress

Per-sport CTL tracking against target values. Countdown to race day with goal completion percentage.

🤖

Claude AI Analysis

Daily AI recommendation powered by Claude. Considers recovery, load, planned workouts and yesterday's DFA data.

🌙

Evening Digest

Daily summary at 21:00 — completed workouts, DFA analysis, tomorrow's plan. Delivered via Telegram.

How It Works

1

Data from Intervals.icu

Wellness, activities and workout plans sync automatically every 10-30 minutes.

2

Analysis & Metrics

Dual-algorithm HRV, RHR baselines, recovery score (0-100), DFA alpha 1 from FIT files, ESS/Banister model.

3

AI Recommendation

Claude evaluates all data and delivers a specific recommendation: what to train, which zones, target volume.

4

Telegram + Dashboard

Morning and evening reports via Telegram. Detailed analytics in the web dashboard.
